# improve-copy — Improve Website Copy

Analyse and improve the written content on the BuildByKey website using conversion copywriting principles.

## How to use
`/improve-copy` — full copy review with suggestions
`/improve-copy [section]` — focus on a specific section (e.g. `/improve-copy hero`)
`/improve-copy apply` — review AND directly apply improvements to index.html

---

## What This Does

You will act as the copywriter agent and review all visible text on the BuildByKey website.

### Brand Voice Reminder
- Confident, direct, specific — like a skilled expert friend
- Small business owner audience — skeptical, busy, wants results
- Active voice always
- No clichés: "seamless", "robust", "leverage", "cutting-edge", "game-changing"
- Lead with outcome, not process

### Copy Review Scope

**Hero section**
- Does the headline instantly communicate the outcome? (not the service)
- Is the subheading specific enough? Does it name a real pain point?
- Are the CTAs action-oriented with a clear next step?

**Services**
- Does each service description lead with the problem it solves?
- Are deliverables specific? (not "we build your website" but "5-page site with CMS")
- Would a busy business owner immediately understand if this is for them?

**Process**
- Is each step name a verb-driven outcome, not just a label?
- Does the process reduce anxiety about hiring someone new?

**Pricing**
- Does the pricing copy frame the investment, not just list the price?
- Is the "Most Popular" badge justified by copy around it?
- Are the feature descriptions benefits, not just features?

**About / Founder**
- Is Tomasz's story specific and credible?
- Does it explain WHY he started BuildByKey (not just WHAT he does)?
- Does it clearly differentiate from agencies?

**Contact**
- Does the contact section reduce friction (response time, no obligation)?
- Is the email prominent and clickable?
- Does the form have the right fields (not too many, not too few)?

### Output Format

```
## Copy Review — BuildByKey

### Overall Grade: [A / B / C / D]
[One-sentence assessment]

### Section-by-Section

#### Hero
**Current:** [existing headline]
**Issue:** [what's weak]
**Rewrite:** [improved version]

#### [Next section...]

### Quick Wins (apply immediately)
- [Change] → [New text]

### Bigger Rewrites (needs discussion)
- [Area] → [Recommendation]
```

If `/improve-copy apply` is called, edit `index.html` directly with the improved copy and report what was changed.
